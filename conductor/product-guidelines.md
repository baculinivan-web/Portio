# Product Guidelines

## Communication Tone
- **Encouraging and Supportive:** The application should maintain a positive and motivating voice, providing reinforcement to help users reach their nutritional and fitness goals.

## Visual and Interaction Design
- **Apple Human Interface Guidelines (HIG) Adherence:** The design must strictly follow HIG principles to ensure a native and intuitive iOS experience.
- **Liquid Glass Aesthetic:** Implement a modern "Liquid Glass" feel using `ultraThinMaterial` for cards and backgrounds, ensuring consistency throughout the app.
- **Subtle and Smooth Animations:** Interactions should be enhanced with fluid transitions and feedback animations that feel natural and responsive.

## User Feedback and Reliability
- **Contextual Feedback:** Utilize haptic feedback and clear visual cues to acknowledge successful actions or alert the user to errors.

## AI Transparency and Trust
- **Subtle AI Identification:** Clearly but unobtrusively indicate AI-generated data (e.g., using a subtle sparkle pictogram) to build trust while maintaining a seamless user interface.
- **Verifiable Results:** Always provide users with the ability to review and edit AI-provided nutritional data to ensure accuracy and user agency.
